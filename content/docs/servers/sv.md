## The Farmworld

Since we don't reset the Overworld we have a Farmworld which gets reset (and regenerated when the worldgen of the pack changes) :br
You can access it by running `/warp farmworld`

::row
  :::card
  ## Worldborders

  Most(!) dimensions have a world border of 10k! (-5000, 5000 to 5000,-5000) :br
  There are no exceptions this time
  :::

  :::card
  ## Dimension Resets

  The following dimensions will reset every Monday at 13:00 CEST:

  - The Nether
  - The End
  - The Farmworld

## Notes

Please do keep in mind that loot chests in the world are per-player, per-chest so everyone has their own unique loot! :br
Also do keep in mind, that these world borders can always be expanded if we need more space!
